Our story

In 2014, we founded "The Jade Rabbit" in Sydney, a company focused on selling ICT equipment and computer products. By accurately targeting market needs,
providing quality customer service and actively participating in community activities,"The Jade Rabbit" quickly won the love and trust of consumers.
Today, it is the market leader in selling ICT equipment and computer products in Australia, not only with a number of physical stores, but also its online platform is popular with customers.

Our Mission

We are committed to promoting digital inclusion and ensuring that all people can enjoy the benefits of scientific and technological advances;
At the same time, through excellent customer service and support, establish long-term customer relationships and become a trusted technical partner of customers.
In addition, the company is committed to operating sustainably, reducing its impact on the environment, and actively participating in community building to contribute positive energy to society.
